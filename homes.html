What We Provide

Services at Sheltering Arms

Every service is delivered with a personal touch, customized to each resident's individual needs and preferences.

ADL Assistance

Full support with activities of daily living: bathing, grooming, toileting, teeth brushing, eating, and dressing, all delivered with dignity.

Medication Management

Certified medication administration by trained caregivers, ensuring every resident receives the right medication at the right time.

24/7 Caregiver Staffing

Around-the-clock care from passionate, CPR-certified caregivers. Someone is always there when your loved one needs them.

Private & Shared Rooms

Both private and shared room options available across our two homes, tailored to your loved one's preferences and needs.

All Meals & Snacks

Fresh, home-cooked meals prepared by our dedicated caregivers, customized weekly to each resident's tastes. Fresh fruits, baked goods, and snacks always available.

Entertainment & Activities

Movies, games, daily activities, and weekend community outings. Because a full life doesn't stop at any age.

Welcome Basket

Every new resident receives a personal welcome basket: a warm first impression that sets the tone for everything that follows.

Personalized Care Plans

No two residents are alike. Every care plan is individualized, because your loved one deserves care designed specifically for them.

Community Outings

Weekend trips to local parks, coffee shops, and ice cream parlors, along with personal trips available with family consent.
