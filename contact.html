Notice of Nondiscrimination

Sheltering Arms Care Homes does not discriminate and does not permit discrimination — including bullying, abuse, or harassment — on the basis of actual or perceived race, color, religion, national origin, ancestry, age, gender, physical or mental disability, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, or HIV status, in accordance with Nevada Revised Statutes § 449.101.

A patient or resident who has experienced prohibited discrimination may file a complaint with the Division of Public and Behavioral Health (DPBH).
