Our Mission

To provide the kind of care for your loved one that we would want provided for our loved ones.

Trusting someone to provide care for your loved one is not a responsibility we take lightly. We strive to provide a safe, healthy, and comfortable environment — just like what we would want for our own family. We thank you for putting your trust in us, and we look forward to exceeding your expectations.

Our Vision

A Place That Feels Like Home

We want to create an environment where our residents feel at home — where they feel safe, where they feel free to be as comfortable or active as they choose, and where they have multiple options to feel a part of the community.

Our Promise

Sheltering Arms abides by all state and federal laws and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, age, disability, gender, sexual orientation, or any other protected characteristic. Every resident is welcomed with dignity and respect.
